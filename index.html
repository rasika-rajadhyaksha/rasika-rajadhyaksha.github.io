About Me

The Story So Far

I work at the intersection of data, strategy, and human behavior.

My career began in analytics, where structured thinking, systems, and numbers shaped the way I approach problems. Over time, curiosity about the people behind those numbers led me into brand and communication strategy, working with clients in FMCG, beauty, real estate, and financial services and other categories. An MBA in Marketing further strengthened my understanding of consumer psychology, positioning, and long-term brand building.

Drawn to complex questions and evolving markets, I’m particularly interested in building brands that combine clarity, cultural relevance, and sustainable growth — strategies that are both commercially sound and meaningfully resonant.

Outside of work, I’m a trained classical singer, a painter, an avid reader, and lifelong learner, constantly exploring ideas across business, psychology, and culture but also can be often found scrolling through social media as much for insight as for entertainment.
